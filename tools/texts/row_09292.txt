article: By . Alexandra Williams . A British cyclist has been killed on one of the best known roads in the Alps after a collision with a motorist, who was also British. Joe Pether, 53, was cycling down the steep descent from the French mountain resort of Alpe d’Huez on a stretch of road famed for its 21 hairpin bends and is often part of the Tour de France route. Mr Pether, from Lower Sunbury, Surrey, collided head-on with the car at one of the road's sharp turns on Sunday evening.
