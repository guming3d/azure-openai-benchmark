When we buy a toasted sandwich maker or a coffee machine we might truly believe they will save us money on costly takeaway snacks and lattes. The reality is that every toastie and coffee we make costs us £10.68, because such gadgets are used so rarely before being put in a cupboard to gather dust. Two out of three households own appliances that have been used an average of six times, while 10 per cent are never touched, research has revealed.
