New York City public school student Zachary Maxwell, now 11, started filming his school lunches in the fall of 2011 because of a dispute with his parents about whether or not he should be allowed to take lunch to school. Over a six month period, Zachary sneaked a video camera into his school cafeteria and filmed his less-than-appetizing meals.
