article: By . Sadie Whitelocks . A new app allows users to assess their love life at a glance by highlighting positive and negative patterns on a color-coded calendar. Users of The Boyfriend Log are instructed to write a brief sentence summarizing how they feel about their relationship each day, marking it green for 'amazing', orange for 'happy', blue for 'sad' and red for 'angry.' If their calendar shows more red than green, the app's creator, 49-year-old Linda Sivertsen, says it's probably time to call it quits and find someone who will have a more positive impact.
