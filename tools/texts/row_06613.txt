Columbus Crew midfielder Kirk Urso died early Sunday morning at a central Ohio hospital without a cause of death known, a county coroner said. Urso, 22, was rushed by ambulance from a downtown Columbus bar to the Grant medical center at 12.50am where he was pronounced dead at 1.50am, The Columbus Dispatch reports.
