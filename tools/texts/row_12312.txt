The Oscar nominee, 26, attended then Schiaparelli and Prada: Impossible Conversations . exhibition . ball on Monday night with her new husband Marcus Mumford. Oxfam, an international relief and development organisation that aims to . create lasting solutions to poverty, hunger, and injustice.
