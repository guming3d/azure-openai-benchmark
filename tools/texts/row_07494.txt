Dave Furukawa, the visually-impaired owner of the 80-pound black boxer, was walking his son, Will, to school when the accident happened. As they crossed Brownwood Avenue in southeast Atlanta, witnesses saw a red Chrysler run a stop sign and strike Simon, the guide dog, then Furukawa. Scroll down for video... They said that Simon's protective instinct kicked in, despite injuries that would prove to be fatal. ‘Simon either got up or jumped out, and pushed my son out of the path of the car,’ Furukawa told WXIA.
